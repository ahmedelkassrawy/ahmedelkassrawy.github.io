Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Shutterabia Publishing Automation application ("the App", "we", "us") accesses and handles data when it publishes content to Facebook Pages and Instagram Business accounts on behalf of the businesses that authorize it.

What the App does

The App is a private social-media publishing tool. It creates, schedules, and publishes posts, reels, and stories to Facebook Pages and Instagram Business accounts that a business has explicitly authorized it to manage. It is operated by the business and its designated social-media managers — it is not a consumer product and does not offer public sign-up.

Information we access

  • Facebook Page and Instagram account content and metadata — the Pages and Instagram Business accounts you connect, and the posts, media, and engagement data needed to publish and manage content on your behalf.
  • Content you provide — captions, images, videos, and scheduling details you or your social-media manager create for publishing.
  • Access tokens — the credentials issued by Meta that authorize the App to publish to your accounts.

We only access the permissions required to publish and manage the content you ask us to publish. We do not access personal profiles, private messages, or unrelated data.

How we use information

Accessed information is used solely to create, schedule, publish, and manage social media content on the accounts you have authorized. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties, and we do not use it for advertising or profiling.

How information is stored

Content and scheduling data are stored on servers operated by us for the sole purpose of publishing. Access tokens are stored securely on the server and are never exposed in logs, dashboards, or to any third party. Access is limited to authorized operators of the App.

Data retention and deletion

We retain content and scheduling data only as long as needed to provide the publishing service. A business may disconnect its accounts at any time, which revokes the App's access. To request deletion of data associated with your accounts, contact us at the address below.

Third-party platforms

The App interacts with Meta Platforms (Facebook and Instagram) through the Meta Graph API. Your use of those platforms is governed by Meta's own privacy policy.

Contact

For any questions about this policy or to request data deletion, contact: shutterabia@gmail.com.
